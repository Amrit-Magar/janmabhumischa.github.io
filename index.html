About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Real Estate

Janmabhumischa Real Estate Pvt. Ltd. was founded with one mission: to make finding land and buildings in Nepal simple, transparent, and stress-free. Based in Kathmandu and serving clients across the country, we bridge buyers, sellers, and investors with the perfect property match.
